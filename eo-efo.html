Executive Order on Efficient Federal Operations

 Home / Policy / Executive Order on Efficient Federal Operations

The President's Executive Order on Efficient Federal Operations, signed on May 17, 2018, directs Federal agencies to manage their buildings, vehicles, and overall operations to optimize energy and environmental performance, reduce waste, and cut costs.

Read the Executive Order and White House Fact Sheet on whitehouse.gov.

Additional information and resources related to Executive Order implementation will be posted shortly.
