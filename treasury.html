Federal Agency Progress Data and OMB Scorecards

 Home / Performance Data / U.S. Department of the Treasury Agency Progress

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's scorecard and historical performance toward energy and sustainability goals are featured in the following categories: Energy and Water Use; Renewable Electricity; Facility Efficiency Investments; High Performance Sustainable Buildings; Fleet Petroleum and Alternative Fuel; and Greenhouse Gas Emissions.

OMB Scorecard for Efficient Federal Operations/Management

By statute, the Director of the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) is required to publish agency progress and scorecards on Federal agency performance on energy efficiency and sustainability.

Download the U.S. Department of the Treasury's FY 2017 OMB Scorecard for Efficient Federal Operations/Management (PDF).
