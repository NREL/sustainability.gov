Federal Agency Progress Data and Sustainability Scorecards

 Home / Performance Data / Social Security Administration

The Social Security Administration's sustainability scorecard and historical performance towards energy and sustainability goals are featured in the following categories: Energy and Water Use; Renewable Electricity; Facility Efficiency Investments; Fleet Petroleum and Alternative Fuel; and Greenhouse Gas Emissions.

Agency Scorecard

By statute, the Director of the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) is required to publish agency progress and scorecards on Federal agency performance on energy efficiency and sustainability.
