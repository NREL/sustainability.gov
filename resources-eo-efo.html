Resources for Executive Order on Efficient Federal Operations

 Home / Policy / Resources for Executive Order on Efficient Federal Operations

Executive Order (E.O.) on Efficient Federal Operations was signed on May 17, 2018. Click on the topics below for links to relevant U.S. codes, public laws, guidance, and resources to assist agencies in implementing the Executive Order. Pursuant to Section 3 of the Executive Order, guidance may be under review and may be revised to facilitate implementation of the E.O. on Efficient Federal Operations.

Facility Energy Efficiency

E.O. Sec. 2(a): Achieve and maintain annual reductions in building energy use and implement energy efficiency measures that reduce costs.
