Presidential Sustainability Executives Program

 Home / Federal Sustainability Plan / Federal Sustainability Plan

Answering President Biden's call to think boldly to meet this "once-in-a-generation" opportunity and as announced through the President's Federal Sustainability Plan, the Biden-Harris Administration is working to bring world-class talent into the Federal sector through a newly launched Presidential Sustainability Executives Program.

This initiative brings leaders from across the public, private, and non-profit sectors into Federal agencies on a limited-term basis to help lead and implement transformational climate solutions across Federal operations, including but not limited to transitioning Federal fleets to zero-emission vehicles, increasing an agency's use of carbon pollution-free electricity, and driving further building electrification.

Next Steps

The Office of the Federal Chief Sustainability Officer is working with Federal agencies to identify challenges that need to be addressed to make significant progress towards the Federal Sustainability Plan's goals. Some sustainability and climate goal areas that could benefit from "out of the box" thinking include:

  • Transition to a zero-emission vehicle fleet
  • Development of a roadmap to prioritize and shift to carbon pollution-free electricity
  • Program review and recommendations to greening supply chains
  • Analysis of critical vulnerabilities and implementation of adaptation strategies
  • Oversight of Agency-defined projects to decarbonize buildings

Federal agencies will work to identify the challenge(s) they want the Presidential Sustainability Executive (PSE) to address and then will work to identify candidates to fulfill the PSE role.

Program Terms and Requirements

PSEs will be embedded within a Federal agency for a term of six months to two years. The Office of the Federal Chief Sustainability Officer will augment agency programming through check-ins, networking opportunities, and speaker events.

PSEs are professionals coming from outside the Federal Government with a demonstrated expertise and skillset in their respective fields, a record of professional achievements, and a Bachelor's degree or higher from an accredited institution. PSE candidates will possess the skills needed to succeed at the highest levels of the Federal Government and the ability to work independently and as part of a team.

This program is open to U.S. citizens employed outside of the Federal Government only. Interested candidates may submit a resume and optional references.
