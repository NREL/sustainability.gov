Letter from the President of the United States

 Home / Letter from the President of the United States

To the Federal Workforce:

First, I want to thank you for your service. You are women and men of immense talent and integrity. You could have chosen another career but you stepped up to make our country better.

As our great Nation emerges from a profound public health and economic crisis caused by the COVID-19 pandemic, we face another emergency that threatens our people and communities, public health, and economy. The signs of our warming climate are here and we have a narrow moment to take decisive action to avoid the most catastrophic impacts of climate change.

To protect our way of life and keep global warming below 1.5 ยบ C, I have directed my Administration to transform our country and economy to be sustainable and resilient to the effects of a changing climate. Critical to these efforts is the Federal Government leading the way.

Through the Federal Sustainability Plan, we will turn the challenge of climate change into an economic opportunity. This once-in-a-generation opportunity will allow us to build the clean energy industries of the future in America, reduce pollution, achieve justice, and protect Federal assets so that you can continue to achieve your mission and serve the American people.

Over the coming months and years, we will be taking bold steps that will fundamentally change how we do business, including to how we make our purchasing decisions to how we operate our buildings and fleets to how we account for future climate impacts.

The actions required to meet this mission will be hard. However, we are the United States of America. There is not a single thing we have not been able to accomplish once we set our minds to it. Together, we can and will meet the challenge of this climate emergency.

I invite you to read the Federal Sustainability Plan and look forward to our continued partnership in preserving our climate and way of life for our families and our future.

Sincerely,
President Joe Biden
