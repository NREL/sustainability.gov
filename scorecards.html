Federal Agency Scorecards

 Home / Scorecards

The Office of Federal Sustainability tracks Federal progress on energy and environmental sustainability performance and collaborates with the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to ensure agencies achieve sustainability goals and requirements.

OMB issues agency Sustainability/Energy Scorecards based on established requirements and performance metrics that rate Federal agency sustainability performance.
